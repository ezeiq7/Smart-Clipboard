Hold Ctrl+Shift to instantly preview your last copied item as a floating overlay — without leaving your current app or losing focus.

Smart Paste

Transform text before it lands. Clean smart quotes, strip line breaks, convert to bullet points, UPPERCASE or lowercase — one keystroke.

Ctrl + Shift + V

Copy a comma-separated list and Smart Clipboard detects it automatically. Split into separate clips, a bullet list, or a numbered list instantly.
